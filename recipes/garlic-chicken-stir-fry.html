Garlic Chicken Stir Fry

Description

Garlic chicken stir fry is a popular and versatile dish in which tender pieces of chicken are cooked with a variety of vegetables, spices, and a prominent amount of garlic. The dish is typically prepared by stir-frying the ingredients in a wok or a skillet over high heat, allowing for quick cooking and preserving the flavors and nutrients of the ingredients. Garlic chicken stir fry is loved for its bold garlic flavor, the tender and juicy chicken, and the colorful assortment of vegetables, making it a go-to dish for quick and delicious meals.

Ingrediets

  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • vegetables
  • Sauces
  • Spices
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Preparation: Slice the chicken and chop the vegetables into desired sizes. Mince the garlic and prepare any additional ingredients.
  2. Marination: Marinate the chicken pieces with soy sauce, a bit of sesame oil, and seasoning for some time to absorb the flavors.
  3. Stir-frying:
    • Heat a wok or skillet on high heat, add oil, and stir-fry the marinated chicken until it's cooked and lightly browned.
    • Add minced garlic and stir-fry for a short time until fragrant.
    • Add the vegetables and stir-fry quickly to retain their crunch and vibrant colors.
  4. Sauce and Seasoning:
    • Incorporate soy sauce, oyster sauce, and other preferred spices for seasoning and flavor enhancement.
    • Mix the ingredients well to evenly coat the chicken and vegetables with the sauce.
  5. Finishing Touches:
    • Adjust seasoning with salt, pepper, or additional sauces as needed.
    • Garnish with optional toppings like chopped green onions, red chili flakes, or sesame seeds for an extra flavor boost and appealing presentation.
  6. Serving: Serve the garlic chicken stir fry hot over steamed rice, noodles, or enjoy it on its own as a flavorful and satisfying meal.
